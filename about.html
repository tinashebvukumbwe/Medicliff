    INFRASTRUCTURE

    MEDICLIFF has built a strong asset base and constantly enhances its infrastructure' facilities to ease operation and give the best working environment to its employees.The office is situated in NORTON (Zimbabwe) on 5000 square meters of land. The entire operation has been designed to suit present and future needs, such as the reception lobby with a sitting capacity of more than 25; a 2000 square meter area housing the admin, purchase, billing sections, and manager's cabins. In addition, a 1500 square meter area comprises the stores and delivery section, where one can arrange stock orderly for easy access. Our facility has been inspected and is in compliance with appropriate storage operational conditions and practices. Strict temperature and humidity controls are in place to ensure ideal storage and distribution conditions.
